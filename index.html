About Me

Bio

Leighton is a multidisciplinary artist and Full Stack developer currently based in the Atlanta GA. He specializes in CGI, XR development and design for interaction. He received a BA in New Media Arts and a minor in Architecture from Southern Polytechnic State University (now Kennesaw State) in 2013. Since then he has worked with a number of companies and startups to build identities and creative digital experiences, that allow people to connect with subjects, products and projects in interesting ways. He uses his knowledge in MongoDB, Express, React and Node Full Stack Development to complement the things he creates.
